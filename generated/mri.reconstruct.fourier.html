class mri.reconstruct.fourier.NFFT(samples, shape)[source]¶

ND non catesian Fast Fourrier Transform class The NFFT will normalize like the FFT2 i.e. in a symetric way. This means that both direct and adjoint operator will be divided by the square root of the number of samples in the fourier domain.

Attributes

samples: np.ndarray the samples locations in the Fourier domain between [-0.5; 0.5[.
shape: tuple of int shape of the image (not necessarly a square matrix).
